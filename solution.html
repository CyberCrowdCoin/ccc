Solution Summary

REVE OTT is a customized and white-label mobile OTT solution that allows Communication Service Providers to build their VoIP-based own branded mobile OTT app and launch service on the existing network. Users registered under Service Provider’s brand can communicate with each other through this app over multiple platforms such as Android, iOS, Windows, and PC.

As a service provider, you can launch or upgrade your OTT Communication Services with Instant Messaging (IM), Media Sharing, Audio-Video Calling, Mobile Recharges, and many more value-added features. REVE OTT helps you to drive traffic and grow your user base while retaining existing users.

Adding mobile OTT features, communication service providers can fight the rising competition from top OTT players like Skype, WhatsApp, Viber, and others without bringing any change to their existing business.

Not only service providers, but REVE OTT solution is also useful for landline and mobile operators, who want to create their own communication app with Voice, Video, Virtual Roaming, and other OTT features.

Solution Highlights

White Label & Customizable

Select and create a version, skin, language & settings under your brand to suit your specific needs & gain a competitive advantage over other mobile OTT players in the market.

Best Quality in Low Bandwidth Areas

Empowers users to make crystal clear high definition audio-video calls over any connection, from anywhere even in low bandwidth areas and restricted networks.

Faster Calling & Messaging

Users can enjoy HD voice, video calling, and messaging with attractive emoticons, media sharing, location tracking, and many rich features.

New Revenue Streams

A customized OTT app & service, with your own logo and branding, give you advantages to capitalize on your existing customer base & increase your revenue.
